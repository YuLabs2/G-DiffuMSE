Performance of STGCN on circles and rectangular array in different scenes

Prove the adaptability of STGCN to different arrays and scenes

Conclusion：
1) In the rectangular array of CHiME3, STGCN improves 52% compared with noisy speech, 11% compared with optimal baseline (CNN), and 1 9% compared with UNet-GCN.
2) In the circle array of cocktail party, STGCN improves 28% compared with noisy speech, 13% compared with optimal baseline (CNN), and 15% compared with UNet-GCN.
3) From the experimental results, it can be demonstrated that STGCN improves the quality of target signal on both rectangular and circular arrays, proving that multi-dimensional graph convolution can achieve array topology adaption.
